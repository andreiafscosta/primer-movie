part two

How it works?

Okay, so the time machine is a fragile box.



You turn it on (point A) with a delayed switch, to avoid encountering your double. Leave it for a few hours, come back, get in it (point B), wait a while and get out at the moment it was turned on in the first place.



So if you turn on the box at 9am and then get in at 3pm, you will wait 6 hours and then get out at 9am.

You always have to stay inside the box the amount of time you want to travel back.

That’s essentially it.
You can only go back in time!



No heading for Jurassic times or scooting to the future! Just repeating a day over and taking advantage of what you’ve learnt in the meantime.
