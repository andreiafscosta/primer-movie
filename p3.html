part three

Avoid paradoxes.

In order to avoid a paradox, you need to hide out during the entire time that the machine is on.

Why?

Because if you and your time traveler version interact with one another, or the course of events of the day is altered in any way, then it would be possible that you, the original version, would never get into the box at “point B”.

The original loses his existence as such in the loop, his future is only to enter the box!

This would not rupture the timeline or anything like that, but it would mean that there are two of the same person in this timeline.



Permanently.



And only one identity between them.



So where to hide?
Hotel.

It’s just them, in a room, with an unplugged television and some board games.



There’s nothing to do but wait.
